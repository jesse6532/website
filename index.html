About Me

Hello, my name is Jesse Rozema. I live in Sherwood Park Alberta, and I am 15 years old. I am a setter with Park Elite 16U Black. As a setter and captain, I like being relied on. Because I am a natural leader, my team looks to me in stressful situations because I am vocal and take control. As an offensive setter, opponents see me as a threat, which gives my teammates single blocks.

I started playing volleyball with Park Elite 13U where we finished 3rd at Provincials. In 14U we finished fifth at provincials and 13th at nationals. In Grade 9, I was the captain for my school team. We were dominant, winning every tournament. In 15u at nationals my team climbed 36 spots to win a gold medal.

By the end of the season, I would like to be a deceptive setter, a dominant blocker, touch 11' and have a hard spin serve. My goal is to play post- secondary.

Beyond practice, I do jump training, strengthen my hands with a weighted ball, and watch film. Every chance I get, I am on my grass court in my back yard.

During practice I am helpful with setting up nets, I am intentional with every rep I get, and when coaches are speaking, I am listening. During games I am competitive yet respectful to coaches, athletes, and officials.
