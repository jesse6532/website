About Me

As a setter and captain, I love being the guy everyone looks to and relies on. Because I am a natural leader, my team looks to me in stressful situations because I am vocal and take control. As an offensive setter, opponents see me as a threat, which gives my teammates single blocks.

I started playing with Park Elite 13U where we finished 3rd at Provincials. In 14U we finished fifth at provincials and 13th at nationals. In Grade 9, I was the captain for my school team. We were dominant, winning every game. In 15u at nationals my team climbed 36 spots to win a gold medal. In the fall of 2025, I was invited to train at Brock Daviduk's setter academy, which was a great opportunity to learn from one of the best setter coaches in Canada. I have been invited to the Team Alberta training camp in 2026, and I am excited to development alongside other top players in the province.

By the end of the season, I would like to be a deceptive setter, a dominant blocker, touch 11' and have a hard to pass spin serve. Ultimately my goal is to play post-secondary.
